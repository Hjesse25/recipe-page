Wiener Schnitzel

Wiener schnitzel is a signature German dish that consists of thin cut meat like veal, chicken, or pork breaded and pan fried.

Ingredients

  • Veal (or any subsitute chicken or pork)
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Seasonings: minced parsley, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  • Bread crumbs
  • Butter
  • Lemon
